InPlay — Streaming Movies, Web Series, Shorts & Audio Series

InPlay is an OTT entertainment platform where you can watch original movies and web series, regional Bhojpuri entertainment, quick-bite short videos and exclusive audio series. Content is streamed in HD with adaptive quality, and works on mobile, tablet and desktop as well as through the InPlay Android app.

What you can watch on InPlay

  • InPlay Cinema — full-length original and licensed movies.
  • Web Series — episodic originals with new episodes added regularly.
  • Bhojpuri Zone — dedicated regional movies, music and shows.
  • InPlay Shorts — quick-bite vertical videos and reels.
  • Audio Series — original audio dramas and stories to listen on the go.

You can browse popular titles for free; a low-cost subscription unlocks the full catalog and an ad-free experience. Payments are processed securely via Razorpay.
